# Government Agricultural Schemes — India & Karnataka

## Central Government Schemes

### PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)
- **Description**: Direct income support to all landholding farmer families.
- **Benefits**: ₹6,000 per year in three installments of ₹2,000 each.
- **Eligibility**: All landholding farmer families.
- **How to Apply**: Visit pmkisan.gov.in or nearest CSC center.
- **Documents**: Aadhaar card, land records, bank account details.

### Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)
- **Description**: Comprehensive crop insurance against natural calamities.
- **Benefits**: Insurance for all food crops, oilseeds, and horticultural crops.
- **Premium**: 2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi, 5% for horticulture.
- **Eligibility**: All farmers growing notified crops.
- **How to Apply**: Through banks, CSC centers, or pmfby.gov.in.

### Kisan Credit Card (KCC)
- **Description**: Affordable short-term credit for farming needs.
- **Benefits**: Loan up to ₹3 lakh at 4% interest (with subsidy).
- **Eligibility**: All farmers, sharecroppers, tenant farmers.
- **How to Apply**: Any scheduled commercial bank branch.

### Soil Health Card Scheme
- **Description**: Soil testing and nutrient-based recommendations.
- **Benefits**: Free soil health cards with fertilizer advice.
- **Eligibility**: All farmers across India.
- **How to Apply**: Contact nearest KVK or agriculture department.

### Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)
- **Description**: Improve water use efficiency through micro-irrigation.
- **Benefits**: Subsidy on drip (55%) and sprinkler (45%) systems.
- **Eligibility**: Small and marginal farmers get higher subsidy.
- **How to Apply**: State agriculture/horticulture department.

### National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA)
- **Description**: Promote climate-resilient farming practices.
- **Benefits**: Support for organic farming, soil health, water conservation.
- **Components**: Rainfed Area Development, Soil Health Management.

### e-NAM (National Agriculture Market)
- **Description**: Online trading platform for agricultural commodities.
- **Benefits**: Better price discovery, reduced middlemen.
- **How to Use**: Register at enam.gov.in with APMC market details.

## Karnataka State Schemes

### Raita Siri
- **Description**: Karnataka state scheme for farmer welfare.
- **Benefits**: Financial assistance for crop inputs and equipment.
- **Eligibility**: Karnataka farmers with valid land records.

### Krishi Bhagya Scheme
- **Description**: Rainwater harvesting and farm pond construction.
- **Benefits**: 80% subsidy for farm ponds, polyethylene lining.
- **Eligibility**: Rainfed areas in Karnataka.
- **How to Apply**: District agriculture office.

### Bhoochetana
- **Description**: Soil health improvement and yield enhancement.
- **Benefits**: Free micronutrient supply, soil testing, training.
- **Coverage**: All districts of Karnataka.

### Yeshasvini Health Insurance
- **Description**: Health insurance for cooperative members.
- **Benefits**: Cashless treatment up to ₹5 lakh.
- **Premium**: ₹710 per year.
- **Eligibility**: Members of cooperative societies.
