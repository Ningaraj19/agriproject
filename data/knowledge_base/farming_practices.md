# Best Farming Practices — Sustainable Agriculture Guide

## Organic Farming

### Benefits
- Eliminates harmful chemical residues in food.
- Improves soil health and biodiversity.
- Premium market prices for organic produce (20–40% higher).
- Government support under Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY).

### Key Practices
1. **Composting**: Use FYM, vermicompost, and green manure.
2. **Bio-fertilizers**: Rhizobium for legumes, Azotobacter for cereals.
3. **Biopesticides**: Neem oil, Trichoderma, Beauveria bassiana.
4. **Crop rotation**: Alternate cereal-legume-vegetable cycles.
5. **Mulching**: Reduce water loss and suppress weeds.

### Certification
- Apply through APEDA or state organic certification agencies.
- Requires 3-year conversion period.
- Group certification available for small farmers.

## Water Conservation

### Rainwater Harvesting
- Construct farm ponds (10m × 10m × 3m for 1-acre farm).
- Line with polyethylene to prevent seepage.
- Karnataka Krishi Bhagya scheme provides 80% subsidy.

### Efficient Irrigation
- **Drip irrigation**: 30–60% water savings; ideal for vegetables and fruits.
- **Mulch**: Reduces evaporation by 25–30%.
- **Scheduling**: Irrigate based on soil moisture, not calendar.

## Post-Harvest Management

### Storage
- Dry grains to <14% moisture before storage.
- Use hermetic (air-tight) storage bags to prevent insect damage.
- Clean and fumigate godowns before storage season.

### Value Addition
- Cleaning and grading: 15–20% price premium.
- Processing: flour, oil, spices for higher margins.
- Direct marketing through e-NAM, farmer markets, FPOs.

## Climate-Smart Agriculture

### Adaptation Strategies
1. Choose drought-tolerant or flood-resistant crop varieties.
2. Diversify crops to spread climate risks.
3. Agroforestry: Plant trees on bunds for shade and income.
4. Conservation tillage: Reduce soil disturbance.

### Carbon Farming
- System of Rice Intensification (SRI): Reduce methane emissions.
- Cover cropping: Sequester carbon in soil.
- Biochar application: Improve soil carbon and water retention.
